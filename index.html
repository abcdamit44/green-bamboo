Green Bamboo Pictures is a Media production company specializing in creating visual content in the form of videos and films for multiple platforms.
At GBP we focus on story telling for a target audience that is diverse, well read and has an astute understanding of the larger social, political and cultural issues at hand. This target audience inhabits the online ecosphere and acts as influencers to their co-inhabitants both online and in the physical realm.
Green Bamboo Pictures envisions itself as an enabler to brands, companies, CSR and environmental platforms in reaching out to the their respective target audience through well crafted content strategies and powerful audio visual products.
Each product is created bespoke, inline with our patron’s expectations and follows our key litmus indicator – creativity, simplicity and innovation.
